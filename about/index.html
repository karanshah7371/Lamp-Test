Here's why we build LAMP

Our mission is to make brands feel more confident and fun on social media.

Here's our story of starting LAMP

It all began a while back when we were enthusiastically building our startup. It was a completely different feel.

Being a small team we have to look after many different things.

We have to constantly create a much better product, manage sales and gotta go through a lot of barriers that hindered our foucs from our goal.

One such barrier was managing social media for our brand.

It s like endless content creation and strategizing about what to share when, logging in and out of multiple social accounts to see if we are meeting daily or weekly goals.

And that's not even counting all the time it takes deciding what content to create or post in the first place.

We spend days on our social content but no-one likes it or shares it! Agencies were very expensive and freelancers didn't provide what we exactly wanted.

Like us, there were many brands facing the same problem.

We wanted to solve the problem for all and that's why we created LAMP.

At LAMP we want to take care of all hassle a brand faces, when it comes to social media.

And we do so by creating perfect social media strategy, consitently creating and posting content for you and organically engaging with your community.

That's too by offering simple and cost efficient subscription based plans.

That's us cheering for you, breaking the barriers.

Now when you have good idea why we started LAMP and what we do, please do check our services, or recommend to anyone who needs it.
